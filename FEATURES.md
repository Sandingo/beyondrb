# Full Feature and Change List
## New Features
* Unused Pokémon from beta builds and future generation evolutions, as well as the inclusion of Kanto-exclusive 'mons from GSC (Houndour/Murkrow/Slugma)
* Shiny Pokémon and Shiny Charm for completing the Pokédex
* Beta versions of the Steel/Dark/Fairy types have been included, along with type changes to match each Pokémon's recent versions.
* Expanded post-game, with a new endgame dungeon in Mt. Silver and Gym Leader rematches.
* The Daycare can now sometimes have Baby Pokémon show up! There are some Pokémon that are only obtained this way.
* Hoaxes like Bill's Garden and Mew under the Truck are featured.
* Gender equality: Play as a girl!
* The Amulet Coin and Everstone rewards from FRLG are obtainable, gotten the same way as in that game.
* Bottle Caps and Hyper Training! The later accessable in the post-game.
* PC and Bag now have visible item counts to make it easier to keep track of.
* A modified version of the Mystery Box from KEP appears, obtainable in the Fuchsia Meeting Room.
* HMs can be used in the overworld by pressing A.
* Hold B to put on your Running Shoes!
* BW's Repel System is included.

## Quality of Life
* Bag size increased from 20 to 30.
* Save speed has been increased.
* Bicycle is usable inside gates.
* Fly can be used on Roof maps and in Viridian Forest
* You can now fly to the Pokémon Centers found on Routes 4 and 10.
* Name Rater can rename any Pokémon, regardless of OT.
* Teaching TMs now has less text.
* TMs and HMs now show their name rather than a numeric value.
* The Move UI in battles now displays Attack Damage alongside Type and PP values.
* You can move between Pokémon Summary screens like in modern releases.
* You can view a Pokémon's hidden stats by pressing Select on their summary screen.
* Pressing Select on the party screen lets you swap your Pokémon around.
* Move Deleter + Relearner in Fuchsia and Indigo Plateau respectively.
* Modern Exp. All sharing formula (Still requires 50 Pokémon caught to get)
* Added NPCs and trades to help with version exclusive or once per save Pokémon.
* Tradeback NPC in Celadon.
* When buying/selling/tossing, you can increment in factors of 10 by pressing Left or Right.
* In Wild Battles, pressing B will automatically hover over the Run option.
* 'Running' in a Trainer battle can be used to Forfeit. Good for Solo Challenges!
* Game Corner Coin prices have been changed to 500 for $1000
* Slot Machine cash outs have been massively buffed.
* A new set of purchasable TMs are in the Game Corner Prize Room.
* The Daycare has a PC available.
* A Nurse NPC has been added to the SS Anne.
* Spin tiles are much faster.
* You will now be notified when a PC Box is completely full.
* When switching boxes, the icon for if the Box has Pokémon or not will be crossed out if it's completely full.
* Safari Zone's step counter now doesn't increment inside the rest houses, and the Step Counter is increased from 500 to 600.
* Vitamins have been buffed, now only requiring 5 to max out a single stat.
* Purchasable PP Ups and Max Revives!
* You can return to the Vermilion Dock even after the SS Anne departs.
* One-use items, such as the Lift Key and Secret Key are removed from the inventory after using them. Less clutter!
* The Itemfinder now points you to the direction of hidden items.

## Changes
* Several maps were altered to match earlier builds of RG, or utilize tiles that were otherwise unseen by the player.
* Locations like the Power Plant and the Fighting Dojo use unique palettes.
* Some palettes and tiles have been altered to reduce eye strain, like the repeating tiles in the Pokémon Mansion being less opaque.
* Some moves have been altered to be more usable, all changed are listed in the [**Google Sheets**](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1M3bosRwThGeiIzk041AlRherJHYMNcCBpjoqek_yNrA/edit?usp=sharing).
* Teleport, Roar and Whirlwind all have their modern mechanics. Phasing is now a thing in RB!
* Unique SGB Borders based on the early designs for it are in.
* The Rocket Hideout trainers have been buffed to not make them completely worthless.
* The Ghost Marowak cannot be skipped with the Pokédoll glitch. There's some unique text to reference this in the battle.
* Obedience has been reworked to be relative to the number of badges the player has rather than relying on specific badges obtained.
* In the post-game, Celadon's Mart carries an expanded variety of TMs and evolution items.
* Aerodactyl is revived using a Wing Fossil. The Old Amber now carries the DNA of a different Pokémon...
* Low-level gift Pokémon have their levels increased to make them viable.
* Sabrina gives out the TM for Light Screen rather than Psywave.
* Pokémon have marginally improved learnsets, including Signature Moves from later generations, learning certain moves on evolution (like Eeveelutions getting a STAB attack), or can access TM/HMs they can learn in Gen 2+
* The Pokédex and Town Map better resemble their JP counterparts.

## Bugfixes
* Focus Energy actually works as intended, increasing your Critical Rate instead of lowering it.
* Nidorino's cry plays in the Oak Intro instead of Nidorina's.
* You can no longer move your moves around while TRANSFORMED, preventing crashes and game breaking bugs from occurring.
* The Rival's win quotes now properly appear when the player loses to him.
* Pokédex now remembers your last viewed Pokémon.
* PP Restoratives will account for PP Ups used.
* Trainer sprites should have less screen tearing.
* Animations that shake the screen, like Psychic, now cover the top couple pixels of the screen.
* Player will turn to face NPCs that trigger a battle with them.
