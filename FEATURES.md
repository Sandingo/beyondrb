# Full Feature and Change List
## Quality of Life Changes

* Bag size increased from 20 to 30.
* Save speed has been increased.
* Hold B to put on your Running Shoes!
* Bicycle is usable inside gates.
* Fly can be used on Roof maps and in Viridian Forest
* You can now fly to the Pokémon Centers found on Routes 4 and 10.
* Name Rater can rename any Pokémon, regardless of OT.
* Teaching TMs now has less text.
* TMs and HMs now show their name rather than a numeric value.
* The Move UI in battles now displays Attack Damage alongside Type and PP values.
* You can move between Pokémon Summary screens like in modern releases.
* Move Deleter + Relearner in Fuchsia and Indigo Plateau respectively.
* Modern Exp. All sharing formula (Still requires 50 Pokémon caught to get)
* BW's Repel System is included.
* HMs can be used in the overworld by pressing A.
* Added NPCs and trades to help with version exclusive or once per save Pokémon.
* Tradeback NPC in Celadon.
* When buying/selling/tossing, you can increment in factors of 10 by pressing Left or Right.
* In Wild Battles, pressing B will automatically hover over the Run option.
* 'Running' in a Trainer battle can be used to Forfeit. Good for Solo Challenges!
* Game Corner Coin prices have been changed to 500 for $1000
* Slot Machine cash outs have been massively buffed.
* A new set of purchasable TMs are in the Game Corner Prize Room.
* The Daycare has a PC available.
* A Nurse NPC has been added to the SS Anne.
* Spin tiles are much faster.
* You will now be notified when a PC Box is completely full.
* When switching boxes, the icon for if the Box has Pokémon or not will be crossed out if it's completely full.
* Safari Zone's step counter now doesn't increment inside the rest houses, and the Step Counter is increased from 500 to 600.
* Vitamins have been buffed, now only requiring 5 to max out a single stat.
* Purchasable PP Ups and Max Revives!
* The floor tiles in the Pokémon Mansion have been made less painful to reduce eye strain.

## Bugfixes

* Focus Energy actually works as intended, increasing your Critical Rate instead of lowering it.
* Nidorino's cry plays in the Oak Intro instead of Nidorina's.
* You can no longer move your moves around while TRANSFORMED, preventing crashes and game breaking bugs from occurring.
